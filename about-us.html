About Us

Welcome to Sweethearts, a leading home health care and nurse registry company committed to providing compassionate and personalized care to our clients.

At Sweethearts, we understand the importance of high-quality healthcare services, and we are dedicated to delivering exceptional care to individuals and families across South Florida and the Suncoast area. Our team of experienced HHAs, CNAs, LPNs, nurses and healthcare professionals are trained to provide a range of services, including in-home care, community-based, and nursing registry services.

We take pride in our ability to match clients with the most qualified and caring professionals in the industry. Our rigorous hiring process ensures that all of our staff members are fully licensed, insured, and thoroughly vetted to meet the highest standards of quality care.

At Sweethearts, we believe that every client deserves individualized attention and care. We work closely with each client to create a personalized care plan that meets their unique needs and preferences. Our goal is to provide comprehensive care that improves the overall health and well-being of our clients and their families.

Whether you are seeking in-home care services for a loved one, or you are a healthcare professional looking for new job opportunities, Sweethearts is here to help. We are committed to providing the highest level of care and support to our clients and staff members alike.

Thank you for choosing Sweethearts as your healthcare and nurse registry provider. We look forward to serving you and your loved ones with the utmost care and compassion.
